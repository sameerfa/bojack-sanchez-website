About Us

Bojack Sanchez is an experimental AI studio that creates podcasts and curated audio experiences. Right now, its public work includes two bite-sized news shows: News in a Nutshell, a global news podcast in English, and Kurz und Klar, a regional German news podcast designed to help non-native speakers improve their German.

Intro

Bojack Sanchez is the parent studio behind a small set of audio projects. The focus is on making useful listening experiences that are clear, concise, and easy to fit into everyday life.

The studio is AI-assisted, but not AI-only. Technology helps with editing and production support, while human judgment shapes what gets published and how it is presented.

What the studio publishes right now

At the moment, Bojack Sanchez publishes two public projects. News in a Nutshell is a bite-sized global news podcast in English. Kurz und Klar is a bite-sized regional German news podcast made for non-native speakers who want relevant news in simpler, more accessible German.

Both shows are built around the same idea: helpful audio that respects your time and makes it easier to stay informed or keep learning without overwhelm.

Why it exists

There is a lot of audio and a lot of information, but not always a lot of clarity. Bojack Sanchez exists to create focused listening experiences that are easier to understand, easier to finish, and more useful in real life.

For the current news shows, that means helping people stay up to date quickly, especially when they do not have time for long episodes, long articles, or endless scrolling.

How it works

The studio uses AI as a production tool, not as a replacement for editorial responsibility. AI can help with drafting, structuring, editing, and presentation so each piece is clearer and more efficient to produce.

Final judgment stays human-led. Source selection, quality control, and publishing decisions are reviewed carefully so the output stays useful, understandable, and trustworthy.
