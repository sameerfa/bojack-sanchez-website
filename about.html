About News in a Nutshell

The story behind the podcast

The Mission

I've always wanted to stay updated with the news and share it in a way that's easy and engaging, but juggling that with a full-time job isn't easy. So, I built News in a Nutshell Podcast - a simple way to break down the biggest stories, twice a day.

I source news from credible outlets, and AI helps edit and present it based on an algorithm I developed. It's my way of staying informed, and I hope it benefits you too.

The Process

Every episode is carefully crafted using a unique algorithm that sources news from credible outlets worldwide. The AI assists in editing and presenting the content, but the editorial decisions and quality control remain firmly human.

Each episode is designed to be consumable in under 2 minutes, perfect for your commute, workout, or coffee break.

The Host

Hi, I'm Bojack Sanchez. I'm passionate about keeping people informed without overwhelming them. In our fast-paced world, I believe everyone deserves access to the news that matters, delivered in a format that respects your time.

When I'm not working on the podcast, I'm exploring new technologies and finding better ways to process and present information.
